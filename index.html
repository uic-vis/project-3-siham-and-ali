Which school is best for my child?

A comprehensive study performed using the City of Chicago crimes dataset

Authors: Siham Shweikani, Aliasgar Zakir Merchant

Selecting a school to send their young ones to is one of the most crucial decisions in parents' lives. School life is an integral part of children's lives and is vital for their academic and personal growth. While making this decision, parents consider a variety of factors. These include:

  • Academic Programs
  • Quality of academic personnel
  • Safety
  • Extracurricular opportunities
  • Location
  • Cost

In light of the rise in school shootings in recent years (on a 2022 study), we decided to evaluate schools in the City of Chicago School District based on the crimes around these schools to assist parents in their decision-making process.

Let's explore the total variation of crimes across the communities of Chicago for the last 5 years (2017-2022).

  • On the left, there is a map of the city of Chicago. On the top right corner of the map exists a control panel. Using this control panel, you can choose what data needs to be displayed on the map. You can click on any community you like to update the bar charts and develop insight.
  • On the right, there is a bar chart which will display the different types of crimes along with their count for the selected community.
  • There also exists a comparison chart. This chart will allow you to compare communities that you previously selected with the current one.

Now, that you have explored some communities and perhaps even created a list of possible options, let us explore the schools in these communities and evaluate the crime rates and distribution in the vicinity of each of them.

  • On the top, you will see, 2 drop down menus where you can choose the parameters for the visualiztion.
  • Below these buttons, you will see yet another map of the city of chicago, however this time, when you click on a community, the schools that are present in that community along with the crime distribution will be shown on the map adjacent to it.

Looking at the crimes rates across the country, the government of United States launched the "Project Safe Neighbourhood (PSN)" initiative which brought together the law enforcement agencies at the federal, state, and local level and various researchers and community agencies to create strategic plans in the form of campaigns and school based programs.

The program, launched in 2001, is mainly targeted towards young gun offenders and ex-offenders in the community to raise awarness about the repercussions and punishments associated with gun violence.

In the city of Chicago, the PSN initiative has been launched in neighbourhoods with high gun violence with the aim to reduce gun violence.
